how to use moonchan?

moonchan is a simple bulletin board that allows anyone to post text and images about a bunch of different topics and interests.
from japanese culture, to fitness, to politics. Users that want to post do not need to register an account to post, making this site partially anonymous.
Before posting to moonchan be sure to familiarize yourself with the rules, you can also check our F.A.Q page down at the bottom of the screen.
